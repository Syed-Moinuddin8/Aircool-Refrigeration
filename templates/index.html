Few AC Problems you maybe facing

Is your AC not working as it should? Below are some issues that will help you to understand the issue:

Refrigerant Leak

If your AC is not cooling your home effectively, it could be due to a refrigerant leak or an issue with the compressor. We diagnose and fix cooling issues to restore comfort quickly.

Noisy AC

Is your AC making strange noises? This could indicate a problem with the fan, motor, or other components. We inspect and repair noisy AC units to ensure a quiet, smooth operation.

AC Water Leakage

Water leaks from the AC could be due to a clogged drain line or a frozen coil. We troubleshoot and repair leaks to prevent water damage and keep your home dry.

AC Not Turning On

If your AC won't start, it could be an issue with the thermostat, power supply, or internal wiring. We provide fast diagnostics and solutions to get your system running again.

Poor Airflow

Poor airflow may indicate blocked air filters or issues with the ducts. We clean filters and inspect the airflow system to ensure that your AC cools the space effectively.

Frozen Coil

A frozen coil can severely limit the cooling capacity of your AC. This is often caused by restricted airflow or low refrigerant levels. We repair and defrost the coil for optimal operation.

High Energy Bills

If your energy bills have risen unexpectedly, your AC could be inefficient due to dirt buildup or outdated components. We inspect and clean your system to reduce energy usage and save you money.
